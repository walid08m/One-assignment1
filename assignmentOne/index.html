Frequently Asked Questions

The answers you'll want before you pack your bag.

  • Who can attend?

    DevConf 2026 is open to everyone in software — engineers, designers, students, founders, and hiring teams. No prior conference experience or seniority level required.

  • Will there be workshops?

    Yes. Alongside the main talks, we're running hands-on workshops across two tracks each day, covering topics from systems design to AI-assisted development. Seats are limited and assigned on a first-come basis.

  • Do I need to bring a laptop?

    Bring one if you plan to join the workshops or the hack lounge — you'll need it to follow along. It's optional for keynotes and panel sessions.

  • Is there a dress code?

    None. Come as you are — most attendees keep it casual and comfortable for a long day on your feet.

  • Will sessions be recorded?

    All main-stage talks will be recorded and shared with registered attendees after the event. Workshop sessions are not recorded.
